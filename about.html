Meet the Editor

Who's behind "Get It Write"?

A Pleasure to Meet You!

I am Nia DeYounge, a Stanford & Boston University graduate with a passion for editing and helping others prepare their best written work (look for me on LinkedIn!). I have my undergraduate and masters degrees in International Relations and am proficient in spoken and written Spanish. I have experience in writing and editing newspaper articles, preparing federal grant applications and foundation proposals, reviewing competitive applications for business school, proofreading dissertations, formatting legal briefs, and writing / editing resumes and cover letters.

The Get It Write Process

Using great communication and some "red-lining" I strive to help my clients put their best foot forward. I know that some ideas can be difficult to articulate on paper and that is where I come in to assist. I work closely with my clients to understand both their work and their ultimate goals. Through one-on-one advising and frequent communication, I help them to effectively convey their strengths and objectives in a clear and concise manner.
